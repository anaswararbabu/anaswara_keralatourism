Top Sights

For all the history lovers, Calicut is the perfect place to be. The Pazhassi Raja Museum and Art Gallery is one of the best places to visit in Calicut. Named after the famous ruler Pazhassi Raja who fought the British with the help of tribes, the museum displays a variety of monuments, mural paintings, and old coins.

Thikkoti Light House

Known to have been constructed on the remains of a shipwreck, the Thikkoti lighthouse is one of the best Calicut attractions to explore. The lighthouse is located on the rocky shoreline and offers scenic views of the sea, and several species of nomadic birds.

Mananchira Square

The Mananchira Square features a clearwater pool at the centre surrounded by temples, churches, and ancient homes built all around the square. Apart from that, one must visit the musical fountain, open stage, and theatre where regular shows are held to depict the historical lives of kings and palaces.

Beypore

Located in the heart of Calicut, Beypore is a popular ship building port which is known to be one of the busiest fishing harbours of Kerala. Considered to be one of the best places to visit in Calicut, the Beypore beach also happens to be an important commercial center for building the ships.

Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary

The Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary is located among numerous islands near the Kadalundi River that hails from the Arabian Sea. Being a home to both local bird species as well as the migratory birds, this bird sanctuary is indeed one of the mesmerising places
